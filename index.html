Who is Noko?

Noko is your partner, your support system, and your best friend. We turn humble clay into little pieces of joy. Each creation hand-shaped with love, blending soft forms and gentle colors to brighten your space. We believe every object should feel as good as it looks — charming, thoughtful, and made to last. From tiny treasures to statement pieces, our work is here to make your everyday a little more special.

Make your own Noko

At Noko, we love bringing your ideas to life. Our custom creations are made just for you — whether it’s a special gift, a unique home piece, or something you’ve been dreaming of for years. We work closely with you to shape every detail, blending our craft with your vision. Each piece becomes a little story you can hold in your hands.
